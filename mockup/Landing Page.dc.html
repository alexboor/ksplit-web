Features

Create a Group

Start a group for any trip or occasion in seconds. Name it, set it up, and you're ready to go.

Invite Members

Add friends with a simple link. Everyone joins instantly and stays in the loop from day one.

Add Expenses

Log who paid, how much, and split equally or with custom amounts per person. Equal or custom — your call.

View Balances

A real-time view of who owes whom and how much. Always crystal clear, no spreadsheets needed.

Record Settlements

When a debt is paid, mark it as settled and keep a clean history of every transaction. Peace of mind for everyone in the group.
